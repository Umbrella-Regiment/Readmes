# Foxhole Umbrella Regiment Leadership Structure Proposal

**Status:** Final Proposal (Consolidated)
**Purpose:** Establish a scalable, resilient, and flexible leadership structure for an Umbrella Regiment
**Philosophy:** Hybrid (Casual + Competitive)

---

# Design Goals

This structure is designed to:

- Scale from **5–10 players** to large-scale wars
- Support **variable availability** of leadership
- Maintain **clear chain of command**
- Allow **delegation and redundancy**
- Support **facility-heavy gameplay**
- Enable **fast decision-making during wars**

# Leadership Tiers

## Tier 1 — Command (Permanent)

### Commander
**Type:** Permanent
**Purpose:** Strategic leadership and final authority

**Responsibilities:**
- Define regiment direction
- Final conflict resolution
- Officer appointments
- Maintain regiment philosophy
- Strategic war-level decisions

**Authority:**
- Final decision authority
- Appoints Officers
- Removes Officers if necessary

---

### Officers
**Type:** Permanent
**Purpose:** Operational leadership and continuity

**Responsibilities:**
- Support Commander
- Provide strategic input
- Maintain discipline and standards
- Fill leadership gaps
- Assist with conflict resolution

**Authority:**
- Operational decision authority
- Can temporarily fill Lead roles
- Help appoint Leads

---

# Tier 2 — War Leadership (Elected Each War)

## Department Leads

**Type:** Elected Each War
**Purpose:** Operational leadership for each department

--Refer to **Regiment Roles & Responsibilites.md** for Department Leads and outline of their Responsibilites 

---

# Tier 3 — Assistant Leadership

## Assistant Team Leads (ATLs)

**Type:** Optional / Appointed
**Purpose:** Backup leadership

**Responsibilities:**
- Assist Department Leads
- Fill in when Leads are unavailable
- Support coordination

**Notes:**
- ATLs are not always present
- Optional but encouraged

---

# Tier 4 — Members

## Members

**Responsibilities:**
- Participate in operations
- Follow leadership direction
- Provide feedback

---

# Selection Rules

## Commander
- Permanent
- Selected by founding leadership

## Officers
- Permanent
- Appointed by Commander

## Command Staff
- A reference to the Commander, Officers, and Department Leads

## Department Leads
- Elected each war
- Officers/Commander intervene if needed
- If Lead is inactive, replacement selected

## Assistant Team Leads
- Appointed by Leads
- Optional

---

# Replacement Rules

If a Lead becomes inactive:

1. Lead provides replacement (preferred)
2. Officers appoint replacement
3. Commander resolves if needed

# Operational Principles

### 1. Leads Own Execution
Officers provide guidance but do not micromanage

### 2. Redundancy Over Perfection
Leadership gaps should not stall operations

### 3. Clear Ownership
Each department owns its domain

### 4. Flexible Scaling
Structure scales with player count
