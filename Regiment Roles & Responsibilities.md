# 📜 Regiment Roles & Responsibilities

This document outlines the leadership structure and responsibilities within the regiment. Each role exists to ensure the regiment operates efficiently, supports the faction war effort, and maintains organized operations.

## 👑 `@Commander`

**Role:** Overall leader of the regiment.

The Commander provides strategic direction, coordinates leadership, and ensures the regiment operates effectively across all departments.

**Responsibilities:**
- Set overall regiment goals and priorities
- Coordinate with all department Leads
- Make final decisions on major operations and strategy
- Resolve internal issues and maintain regiment cohesion
- Represent the regiment in external coordination with other groups

## 🏭 `@Lead – Facility`

**Role:** Designs and establishes regiment facilities and their strategic purpose.

The `@Lead Facility` is responsible for the initial planning, layout, and strategic intent of regiment facilities. This includes determining how the facility supports the regiment’s war goals and establishing the infrastructure required to meet those objectives and provide ongoing support to `@Lead - Production`.

**Responsibilities:**
- Design and organize facility layouts and infrastructure
- Determine the initial production focus and intended throughput aligned with regiment strategy
- Establish the structures, power, and logistical access needed to support that purpose
- Coordinate with Defense to ensure the facility can be protected
- Continue to maintain the facility throughout the course of the war and coordinate with the `@Lead - Production` on any facility issues or modifications needed to accommodate changes in goals or production needs
- Hand off operational responsibility to the `@Lead – Production`, who manages the ongoing operation and output of the facility
- Ensure the Facility is supplied and repaired through coordination with `@Lead - Defense` (Msup Tunnels will overlap both roles)


## ⚙️ `@Lead – Production`

**Role:** Oversees the operational management and output of regiment facilities.

The Production Lead is responsible for executing and operating the production goals established during facility planning and throughout the war, converting raw materials into finished goods while maintaining efficiency and meeting operational needs.

**Responsibilities:**
- Operate and manage the facility production chain from raw resources to finished goods
- Determine operational production targets based on regiment needs and planned operations
- Maintain and prioritize production queues
- Coordinate with Logistics for material intake and distribution
- Coordinate with Logistics to support MPF production during major vehicle pushes (e.g., tank wars)
- Monitor facility efficiency and adjust production to meet operational demand
- Coordinate with the `@Lead - Facility` on any facility issues or modifications needed to accommodate changes in goals or production needs
- Train members on facility operation and production processes
- Any and all production based agreements with other Regiments or Individuals need to be vetted and approved by this role

## 🚚 `@Lead – Logistics`

**Role:** Oversees resource movement and supply chains.

The Logistics Lead ensures resources move efficiently from gathering locations to facilities and from production to the front line.

**Responsibilities:**
- Coordinate resource gathering and hauling operations
- Maintain supply routes between facilities and the front
- Monitor stock levels and identify shortages
- Organize logistics runs and convoy operations
- Communicate supply needs with Production and Operations

## 🛡️ `@Lead – Defense`

**Role:** Responsible for all defensive planning and fortifications.

The Defense Lead ensures regiment assets, facilities, and frontline positions are protected with effective defenses.

**Responsibilities:**
- Plan and oversee bunker and defensive structure construction
- Maintain and upgrade existing defensive lines
- Identify weak points and organize improvements
- Coordinate with Facility Lead for base protection
- Ensure defenses are supplied and repaired through coordination with `@Lead - Facility` (Msup Tunnels will overlap both roles)

## 🎯 `@Lead – Operations`

**Role:** Plans and coordinates regiment activities.

The Operations Lead ensures regiment members are organized into meaningful and effective operations.

**Responsibilities:**
- Plan and schedule regiment operations
- Support Regiment members in planning and scheduling operations
- Coordinate Regiment efforts to ensure operation prep is completed for a schedule operation with `@Lead - Production`, `@Lead - Logistics`, etc.
- Coordinate with department Leads to support objectives
- Assign roles during operations
- Encourage participation and maintain activity
- Identify opportunities for regiment involvement in the war effort

## 🤝 `@Lead – Engagement`

**Role:** Manages recruitment and member activity.

The Engagement Lead ensures the regiment continues to grow and maintains an active community.

**Responsibilities:**
- Recruit new members
- Manage onboarding for new recruits
- Promote regiment culture and activity
- Help integrate new players into operations
- Coordinate recruitment needs with Command

## ⚔️ Leadership Expectations

All leadership roles are expected to:

- Communicate clearly with other leaders
- Support regiment operations and events
- Help train and guide members
- Maintain a positive and organized environment
- Act in the best interest of the regiment and faction
